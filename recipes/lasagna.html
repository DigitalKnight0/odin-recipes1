Lasagna

Description

Lasagne (US: /ləˈzɑːnjə/, also UK: /ləˈzænjə/,[1] Italian: [laˈzaɲɲe]; singular lasagna, Italian: [laˈzaɲɲa]) are a type of pasta, possibly one of the oldest types,[2] made of very wide, flat sheets.

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless skinless chicken breasts, cut into 1/4-inch strips
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1 cup salsa
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste

Steps to make

  1. Combine chicken, lime juice, and taco seasoning in a large bowl. Stir to make sure chicken is evenly covered.
